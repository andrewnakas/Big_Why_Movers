Moving best practices

Simple, proven tips to keep your move efficient, safe, and stress‑light — from the first box to the last high‑five.

2–4 weeks out

  • Book your movers early — weekends and month‑ends fill quickly.
  • Confirm elevator/parking availability and any building requirements.
  • Sort and donate — fewer items = faster move and lower cost.
  • Measure larger items and entryways; note stairs or long carries.

Packing like a pro

  • Label top and two sides: room + quick contents.
  • Heavy stuff small boxes; light stuff big boxes.
  • Pad corners and wrap art/mirrors — stand them on edge.
  • Tape properly: two across, one down (the H‑tape).

Be move‑ready

  • Have everything boxed, sealed, and labeled before crew arrival.
  • Empty dressers and cabinets; disassemble beds unless you request help.
  • Unplug, drain, and dry appliances (fridge 24 hours prior if possible).
  • Clear paths and protect floors; reserve parking/loading zones if needed.

Share details & photos

Photos or a short video help us quote accurately and plan the right crew/gear.

  • Count of boxes and totes; note oversized or fragile items.
  • Shots of entryways, stairs, elevators, and any tight turns.

Day‑of checklist

  • Pack an essentials box: meds, chargers, snacks, coffee kit.
  • Empty drawers and secure loose shelves.
  • Keep pets safe in a quiet room or with a friend.
